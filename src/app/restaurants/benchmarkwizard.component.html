Review and Benchmarking

Review allows members to analyse the financial and statistical data associated with their restaurants. Benchmarking allows members to compare those data with data from other restaurants in their portfolio, or with sets of restaurants that have similar characteristics (for example, might be in the same area, have the same primary cuisine, etc.)
